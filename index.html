How CivicSoul Works

Simple steps to make your voice heard and create positive change in your community

1

Submit Your Complaint

Fill out our simple form with details about your civic issue. Upload photos, add location, and describe the problem clearly.

2

AI Processing & Routing

Our Claude AI analyzes your complaint, determines the relevant department, and creates a professional email automatically.

3

Department Notification

The formal email is sent to the appropriate municipal department with all necessary details and documentation.

4

Track Progress

Monitor your complaint status in real-time through your personal dashboard. Get updates via email and SMS.
